What exactly Does Odoo Multi Vendor Marketplace Module Does?

It transforms your single e-commerce website into a working e-commerce marketplace. Your sellers would signup and sell their products.

What your business receives are; A flood of customers from every walk of society and commissions for every sale they make on your website.

You as the Marketplace admin is entitled to complete control on the happenings on your Odoo Marketplace. You approve/disapprove products, orders, sellers, etc, manage sellers' payments, seller shops and much more.

KEY FEATURES
Here are the key features of Odoo Multi Vendor Marketplace.
Separate Marketplace Admin Panel
  • Neat Dashboard for quick peek.
  • Separate menu for sellers and Sellers shops management.
  • Create New Marketplace products.
  • Approve/disapprove new product requests from sellers.
  • Manage product Inventory requests.
  • Manage seller orders.
  • Separate Seller payment Menu.
  • Publish/unpublish seller Ratings and recommendations.
Enhanced Marketplace Website View
  • Marketplace website is designed for easy navigation for the customers.
  • A separate seller shop for each seller.
  • Seller information and shop link visible on the product page.
  • Customers can view seller shop and its details on the website.
  • The customers can buy from their favorite seller directly from his Marketplace shop.
  • Write a Seller review and view ratings from others.
Seller Dashboard Features
  • Elegant Seller Dashboard for faster shop navigation
  • The seller can view and edit their Marketplace shop details.
  • Set shop page, shop policies, etc.
  • Separate menu for monitoring orders and payments
  • Request admin for payments
  • Manage Marketplace products.
  • Send new product and Inventory update requests to admin.
Automatic Mail Notifications
  • Manage automatic Mail notifications from settings
  • Enable mail notifications on new seller requests for both admin and seller
  • Mail alerts on new product requests.
  • approval/disapproval of new requests sends mail to both admin and seller.
  • Enable Alerts on a new order placed on Marketplace.
Commission Management For Admin
  • The Admin commission rules can be set globally or for individual seller.
  • Global Settings apply for all sellers.
  • Global settings can be over-ridden in seller profile by the admin.
  • Earn percentage-based commission for every sale on your Marketplace.
Display Ratings And Recommendations
  • Build authenticity and customer trust with ratings and reviews on your Marketplace.
  • An inherent ratings system in the module. No additional addon required.
  • As the Admin, manage seller ratings and reviews from customers.
  • A separate tab in the Admin Panel for easy management.
  • publish/unpublish ratings on the marketplace website.
GDPR / EU Privacy:

This addons does not collect any data and does not set any browser cookies.
