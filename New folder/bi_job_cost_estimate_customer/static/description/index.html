Job Work Estimation for Material Cost, Labour cost and Overheads Cost Odoo Apps

This Odoo apps helps to calculate job cost and job work estimation for Material, Labour and Overheads Cost which easily send to your Customers as total estimation for the job work order.This odoo apps helps for Project/analytic account job order costing calculation for Material Cost, Labour cost and Overheads Cost. Based on this work order cost estimation user you can also able to create quotation and sales order also its linked with job estimation sheet.This odoo module also helps to manage Job order estimation approval workflow for manager and also send estimation to customer by email before approval to start work order.

Features

Create Job Order Estimation Cost.

Send estimation to customer with material, labour and overhead cost after getting approval from customer,Quotation can be created for that estimation.

Send job Estimation by mail, Print PDF Report.

Send mail to customer and also have feature to print PDF report for job estimation.
Demo Request Video Tutorial

Job Estimation Menu

You can create job estimation from Sales -> Orders -> Job Estimates.

Job Estimation Form View

You can add specific details of job estimation.

Add material information for job estimation.

Add labour information for job estimation.

Add overhead estimate information for job estimation.

You can confirm job estimation by clicking on "Confirm" button.

When you confirm job estimation state is changed from draft to confirm state.

You can Approve or Reject job estimation

When you click on "Approve" button job estimation is approved and state is changed to Approved.

After approving of job estimation you can create quotation for material, labour and overhead products by click on "Create Quotation"button..

Quotation for job estimation is created.

Created Sale Order

Here you can see quotation created from job estimation.

You can also reset to draft job estimation by clicking "Reset To Draft" button

Also job estimation state will be changed to draft state.

You can also send job estimation to client by applying "Send By Mail".

Send By Mail

You can send job estimation to your client through email.

Job Estimation Report view

You can also get report of job estimation.
